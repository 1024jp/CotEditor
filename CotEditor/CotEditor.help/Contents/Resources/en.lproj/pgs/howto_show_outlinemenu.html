How to show Outline

The Outline feature generates a drop-down menu listing specific parts of your document according to the Customize Outline rules of your syntax style. The menu is displayed beneath the toolbar. By clicking on an item in the menu, you can easily go and select the corresponding part.

  • To show Outline Menu, choose View > Show Navigation Bar.
  • You need to make syntax coloring active to use Outline Menu.

The Customize Outline rules, where you can configure what items should appear and how they should be titled in the menu, are included in the Syntax Style settings.
